3. Where your data is stored

On your device (default)

By default, everything you create is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. If you have never enabled cloud backup, your learning data never leaves your device through the Service. You can also export a full JSON backup or auto-back-up to a local folder you choose.

In the cloud (opt-in)

If you turn on Auto Cloud Backup, your entries, goals, achievements, categories, and preferences are synced to our cloud database so they are backed up and available across your devices. Our cloud database and authentication are provided by Supabase, acting as our processor. Authentication uses passwordless email one-time codes or Google sign-in. You can turn cloud backup off at any time.

4. How long we keep it (retention)

  • Active data — kept for as long as your account exists and you use the Service.
  • Deleted entries and goals (recycle bin) — when you delete a log entry or a goal, it is moved to a recycle bin and can be restored. Items in the recycle bin are permanently purged after 90 days.
  • Account deletion — when you request account deletion from the app, your account is scheduled for deletion and enters a grace period of approximately [~60 DAYS — CONFIRM]. If you sign back in during that window, the deletion is cancelled. After the window passes, your cloud data is removed.

Local data stored in your browser remains on your device until you clear it, reset the app, or sign out in a way that clears local account data. Local backups you exported or saved to a folder are under your control and are not deleted by us.

5. How we use your data

  • to provide the core features (logging, goals, analytics, reports, achievements);
  • to authenticate you and keep your account secure;
  • to back up and sync your data across devices when you enable cloud backup;
  • to enforce plan features and profile limits;
  • to maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.
