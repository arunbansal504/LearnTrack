What's inside

Everything a serious learner needs

Built from the ground up for students, self-taught developers, and lifelong learners.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

At-a-glance stats, interactive charts, daily heatmap, streak tracking, and AI-generated insights — all on one screen.

📝

Daily Learning Log

Log every study session with topic, category, duration, difficulty, mood, notes, and resource links. Search and filter instantly.

🎯

Academic Goals

Set time-based, count-based, checklist, or exam goals. Track progress visually and link your log entries directly to each goal.

📅

Calendar View

Browse your entire learning history month by month. Click any day to see exactly what you studied and for how long.

🏆

Achievements & XP

Earn XP for every session, level up your profile, and unlock badges for streaks, milestones, and consistency.

📄

PDF Monthly Reports

Export a detailed monthly report as a PDF in one click — complete with charts, tables, and all your entries formatted cleanly.

⏱️

Pomodoro Timer

Built-in focus timer that auto-creates a log entry when your session ends — so you never forget to record a study block.

👥

Multi-Profile Households

One account, up to five learner profiles. Perfect for families or students tracking multiple subjects separately.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Opt-in cloud backup keeps your data safe and synced across devices. Fully offline-first — everything works without a connection.
